Pillar 01

Content intelligence that sharpens with use.

Every cycle adds signal. After a hundred cycles, Quill knows your brand better than a new hire ever could.

Brand Voice as Living Model
Ingests your full content corpus and builds a voice fingerprint — vocabulary, rhythm, tone. Updates with every published piece.
Performance-Aware Drafting
Learns that data-led openings work on LinkedIn but narrative hooks land on X. Drafts improve because the agent accumulated signal.
Research Beyond Summarisation
Identifies topics with high demand but low quality coverage. Monitors competitors. Surfaces the angle nobody is serving well yet.
Pillar 02

Autonomous operation, not assisted prompting.

Quill doesn't wait. It identifies this week's best angles, drafts, schedules, tests, measures, and adjusts.

Strategic Content Calendar
"You haven't covered this topic in three weeks — it's 30% of your audience's interest." The calendar recommends; it doesn't just fill slots.
Adaptive Scheduling
Learns when your audience is active per platform. Experiments, measures the delta, adjusts. Genuine hypothesis-action-adaptation.
Autonomous A/B Testing
Two headlines. Two hooks. Split across platforms. Publishes both, measures which performs, applies patterns to future drafts — every cycle.
